Investors

How ZEROTH can help

Early stage investments are completely different than investments in blue chip stocks. There are no financials to do historical analysis on and no Price to Earnings ratio to compare against other investments. Early stage technology can be a big risk with a big return, but if you don't know what questions to ask it is mostly a risk that will turn into a loss.

ZEROTH relies on its experience watching early stage technologies fail and succeed to make sure the right questions have been asked before you invest your money in a new technology.

Answers

  • Will it sell?
  • Do the patents cover the features that will sell?
  • Do they have the right team?
  • What are early exit opportunities?
