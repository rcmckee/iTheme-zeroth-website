How ZEROTH can help

Whether you are directing R&D or the Legal Department, ZEROTH can help you make the most of your budget. Your company is developing creative new innovations behind closed doors. With your current process you are spending hundreds of thousands of dollars, if not millions on R&D and patent attorneys before you can find out if the market will buy the new technology. This method leads to patents that are overly broad, vague, and use complex language so that the lawyers can later argue in court that one of the patents cover whatever is valuable.

This causes problems that you might be experiencing but have not had a good solution for, such as:

  • Spending most of your time and money on patent prosecution.
  • Not know which provisional applications to turn into nonprovisional applications when your budget is forcing you to choose.
  • Not knowing which provisional applications to turn into US only or PCT applications.
  • Not knowing which dependent claims are the most important to include in your application.
  • Not knowing what is the most important supporting evidence to include in the patent description.

A lot of the uncertainty is caused by a fear of missing out on valuable technology. At ZEROTH, we help you determine what IS valuable so that you can confidently build a strong patent portfolio from the begining, instead of waiting for a judge in East Texas to decide.
