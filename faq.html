FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What does "Zeroth" mean?
zēˌrōTH (Zero + th) - immediately preceding what is otherwise regarded as first in a series.

Traditionally, getting a patent is regarded as the first step in profiting from innovation. We help you take that first step with confidence by making sure that you are spending your time and money on innovation that will sell.
How will ZEROTH help me before I get a patent?
Not all novel inventions will sell. Not all innovations need a patent. Since the priority date is from the date of filing, we want you to have priority on the most valuable features from the earliest date possible.
How will ZEROTH help me if I already have a patent?
We can help you determine if you should file more applications, if you should cut your losses, what is the best strategy for bringing the technology to market, and if there are any exit opportunities available.
What about confidentiality?
We take confidentiality seriously. We will never disclose client names, how the technology works, or any information you do not wish disclosed. To get you the maximum value from our service we just need to know what your innovation does, not how it does it. Please see our Confidentiality page for more information.
